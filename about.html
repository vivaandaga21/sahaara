About Us

Sahaara is an initiative dedicated to crafting shelters for vulnerable women, prioritizing their safety and well-being. With a commitment to sustainability, we employ innovative cooling mechanisms and eco-conscious materials to build a sense of belonging. Beyond mere shelter, we strive to bestow dignity, instill hope, and nurture a true sense of home.
